About Our Founder

My name is Avanganino Parmagino, and I lead a team of the world’s most talented private chefs. Originally from Italy, I brought my passion for authentic cuisine to the United States to start Tavola Alta®, blending traditional European artistry with modern culinary innovation.

What began as a small private dining service for close friends and families in Milan has evolved into a global network of highly trained chefs serving clients across the world. Each of our chefs is carefully selected for their creativity, discipline, and deep understanding of international cuisines.

Our Mission

We believe dining should transcend the plate — it’s an experience that engages every sense. From intimate dinners to grand corporate events and luxurious week-long retreats, our mission is to craft personalized, unforgettable culinary moments that linger long after the last bite.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: Every dish tells a story rooted in culture and tradition.
  • Excellence: We use only the finest ingredients and techniques.
  • Innovation: We constantly evolve to stay at the forefront of culinary artistry.
  • Hospitality: We treat every client like family.

Meet Our Team

Our chefs come from Michelin-starred restaurants, luxury resorts, and private estates. Together, they bring decades of combined experience in international fine dining, pastry art, and gourmet catering.
